ANALYSIS & INTERVENTIONS

P1 — THE BROKEN FEEDBACK LOOP
01
Field Evidence
Sarah · Elite Host (5 Units)
"جاذر إن بين فترة وفترة يرسلون استبيان... كتبنا ملاحظاتنا مرة وثنتين وثلاث بس مافي تعديل."

Consistent pattern: Hosts invest time in surveys and receive zero visible action. Gathern listens, but never responds.

The CX Reality

A system that collects without closing the loop is a trust-destruction machine. Host disengagement starts silently — lower units, less responsiveness, and fragile loyalty.

The Intervention
  • Implement Closed-Loop VoC as I did at Zid. Result: −17% churn.
  • Launch a Host Feedback Action Report: Monthly transparency on what was actioned and why.
P2 — SUPPORT ABANDONMENT
02
Field Evidence
Abdullah · Community Admin
"الدعم ضعيف جداً في حال احتجت مساعدة أو حصلت لك مشكلة."

When the most experienced hosts warn about support, it's a red flag. Automation is currently failing in real-world emergencies.

The CX Reality

Automation is not a strategy; it's abandonment in high-stakes moments (lockouts, property damage). Bad stories travel to 43k members instantly.

The Intervention
  • Tiered Support Model: Clear escalation paths to human agents for flagged emergencies.
  • Priority Routing for High-Value Hosts (3+ units or 4.5+ ratings).
P3 — NO HOST PROTECTION
03
Field Evidence

The CX Reality

Slow dispute processes signal that Gathern prioritizes guests over host fairness. A host feeling unprotected will not add Unit 13; they switch to competitors.

The Intervention
  • Time-Bound Protocol: Acknowledgment within 24h, final decision within 5 business days.
  • Transparent Criteria: Define exactly what qualifies as removable to eliminate guesswork.
